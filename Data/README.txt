heart_binarized.csv - Dataset 1
Binarized version of our heart disease dataset retrieved from Kaggle. 1025 samples, and 33 binary attributes, one of which being the target.

anxiety_binarized.csv - Dataset 2
Binarized version of our depression and anxiety dataset retrieved from Kaggle. 784 samples, and 60 binary attributes, one of which being the target.

Medicaid_Conditions_Binarized.csv - Dataset 3
Binarized version of our chronic condition prevalence dataset retrieved from the data center of Medicaid & Medicare services. 29,080 samples, 95 binary attributes, and 1 continuous attribute, which is our target.
